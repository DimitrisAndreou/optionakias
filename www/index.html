This is the only page you need to bookmark to keep track with all my tools!

  • IBKR Realized/Unrealized P&L by Symbol. Interactive Brokers unfortunately still doesn't offer a way to sum up your P&L via a symbol (e.g. the P&L of your AAPL stock, together with all the P&Ls of the options on AAPL). This tool does this.
  • BTC/ETH Options Analyzer (via ByBit's data). Basically this adds extra features to a typical option chain, for example it can explain selling a put as a trade that gives you a certain break even improvement, for agreeing to a bounded max gain (%), versus unlimited upside of buying the underlying on the spot market. Simple things like this can make options more digestable for newcomers.
