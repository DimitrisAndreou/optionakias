This tool lets IBKR users see the realized/unrealized Profit & Loss (PNL) for all their positions, grouped by symbol. I.e. if you traded 50 different option contracts of a particular stock (and maybe traded the stock itself), you will here see a single position, instead of the 50 (or 51) that IBKR would report.

To use this, simply download "Realized & Unrealized" CSV reports provided by IBKR (you can select many). Ideally you should select all CSV since inception of your IBKR account, and make sure the CSV files do not refer to overlapping periods (otherwise the PNL of the overlapping periods will be double-counted).

Note that this script processes the files locally, never sends your files anywhere (it doesn't require an internet connection to work).
